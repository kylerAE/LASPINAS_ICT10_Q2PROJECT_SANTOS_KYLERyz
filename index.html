About Our Ship

Kyler's School of Pirates is a premier educational institution on the Grand Line, dedicated to nurturing young pirates and preparing them for the challenges of finding the One Piece.

With a rich history spanning over the Void Century, we take pride in our tradition of Haki mastery and Devil Fruit research. Our state-of-the-art ships, experienced Yonko faculty, and adventurous curriculum create an environment where students can thrive.
